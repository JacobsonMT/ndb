About MARVdb

Mission statement

The MARVdb web application and database is an initiative aimed at harmonizing different sources of reports for genetic variants. We hope that MARVdb provides the scientific community a comprehensive collection of genetic variants published in peer-reviewed literature.

Involvement

This project is a product built by the Pavlidis Lab of the Michael Smith Laboratories at the University of British Columbia. This project is developed in tandem with other intiatives contributing to the Simons Foundation Autism Research Initiative (SFARI) research projects aimed to better our understanding Autism Spectrum Disorders.
